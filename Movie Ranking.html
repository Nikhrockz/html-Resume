The Best Anime According To Nikhil

My Top 6 Anime of all-time

Bleach

This was my first ever anime and it was no disappointment whatsoever. Therefor it will always have a special place in my heart.

Naruto

Just one of the GOAT .. no explanation needed.

Demon Slayer

This anime has so much quality in terms of image and sound, they really went all out with their budget. The story is also really good.

Jujutsu Kaisen

Just Bleach Vibes

Kaiju No.8

This one you just feel yourself going with the anime. Nice story, cool charcters and alot of action.

Solo Leveling

The next big one following Demon Slayer. EVERYTHING is well done of this anime, a must watch.
